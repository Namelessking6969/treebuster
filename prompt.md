# Tree Busters and More — Full Brand Kit Prompt

## Role
Act as a senior brand strategist, visual designer, and marketing expert.  
Create a complete, cohesive brand identity for a rugged, professional local service business.

---

## Brand Overview

**Brand Name:** Tree Busters and More  
**Industry:** Tree Services, Snow Plowing, Property Maintenance  

**Core Services:**
- Tree cutting & removal  
- Tree trimming & pruning  
- Snow plowing & snow removal  
- Storm cleanup  
- Stump grinding  
- General outdoor & property services

**Target Audience:**
Homeowners, landlords, and small commercial property owners who value reliability, fast response, and professional workmanship.

---

## Brand Personality

- Strong  
- Dependable  
- Hard-working  
- Honest  
- Local & trustworthy  
- No-nonsense, blue-collar professionalism

**Brand Vibe:**  
“Show up, get it done right, and stand behind the work.”

---

## Brand Voice & Tone

**Voice Characteristics:**
- Confident  
- Straightforward  
- Professional but approachable  
- Local and trustworthy  
- Zero fluff, no corporate jargon

**Example Tone:**
- “We answer the phone.”  
- “We show up on time.”  
- “We get the job done safely and right.”

---

## Logo Design System

**Logo Name:** Tree Busters and More  

**Logo Styles to Create:**
1. Primary logo (horizontal)  
2. Secondary logo (stacked or badge)  
3. Icon / mark (for social media, favicon, decals)

**Visual Elements:**
- Trees, stumps, chainsaw or saw blade  
- Snow elements (subtle snowflake, plow blade, winter accents)  
- Shield, badge, or circular emblem (optional)

**Logo Feel:**
- Bold and rugged  
- Clean and readable at distance  
- Works on trucks, trailers, signs, uniforms, and website

---

## Color Palette

Create a primary and secondary color palette with hex codes.

**Primary Colors:**
- Forest Green  
- Deep Blue  
- Charcoal / Near-Black  

**Secondary / Accent Colors:**
- White  
- Light gray  
- Safety orange (very minimal, optional accent)

Include:
- Background usage  
- Text usage  
- Accent/CTA usage

---

## Typography System

**Primary Font:**
- Heavy, bold sans-serif or slab-serif  
- Used for headings, logo text, CTAs

**Secondary Font:**
- Clean, readable sans-serif  
- Used for body text and longer content

Include:
- Font names  
- Font weights  
- Usage guidelines

---

## Iconography & Graphic Style

- Simple, bold icons  
- Easy to understand at a glance  
- Outdoor / industrial style  
- Consistent stroke width and shape

---

## Photography & Visual Style

**Photo Guidelines:**
- Real-world, outdoor work scenes  
- Tree cutting, snow plowing, equipment, crews at work  
- Natural lighting  
- Gritty but professional (not staged stock photos)

**Visual Mood:**
- Early morning / golden hour  
- Snowy winter scenes and green summer scenes  
- Clean equipment, safety gear visible

Design guidelines and mockups for:

**Posting Style:**
- Job photos  
- Before/after shots  
- Snow storm updates  
- Quick service reminders  
- Local community tone

“Storm coming in tonight — we’re ready. Call Tree Busters and More for fast snow removal.”

## Usage Rules

- Logo must remain legible at all sizes  
- Maintain color contrast for readability  
- Do not overuse accent colors  
- Brand should always feel strong, reliable, and professional
